Who’s
Your
Kitchen Daddy?

We are dedicated to delivering innovation and capability to Australian brands and operators.

One recipe, one project, and one pallet at a time.

Fostered by our team of industry professionals and our executive chef, we stand out by using natural ingredients and customising our offerings to meet our customers’ unique needs..

Unlike larger factories that only offer generic solutions, we invest heavily in research and
development to bring your culinary visions to life.

Our team, lead by an executive chef who hails from Tokyo’s finest restaurants, brings exceptional expertise to our recipe development, ensuring premium quality and flavour.

We are proud to be one of the few manufacturers focusing on authentic Japanese cuisine, though we also excel at creating popular dishes from all areas of the globe.

What’s On
The Menu?

We work with our clients to manage the entire food manufacturing process: recipe creation, food production and product packaging. We also provide supply chain consultancy services.

Custom Food Manufacturing Solutions

We specialise in providing high-quality manufacturing solutions for both commercial and institutional food service outlets. Our state-of-the-art facility allows us to deliver shelf-stable, chilled, and frozen food products.

End-to-End Food Manufacturing

We manage from recipe creation to food production and packaging. We offer supply chain consultancy and procurement consulting to ensure your operations run smoothly.

Recipe
Development

We collaborate with you to develop recipes from scratch, ensuring they meet taste, nutrition, dietary, size, and cost requirements. We can scale your existing recipes to maintain consistency and efficiency, whether you need a small batch or several pallets.

Advanced Manufacturing Facility

Our facility is equipped to handle both bespoke and large-scale food production, offering chilled and frozen food solutions tailored to your needs.

Comprehensive Packaging
Solutions

We provide private label and white label food packaging, working closely with you to find the perfect packaging solution while ensuring all FSANZ compliance requirements are met.

Our clients and brands.

Our clients share our dream – good food, made simple! As well as developing client’s products we have a range of retail and wholesale products available – it’s a taste of what we can do!
